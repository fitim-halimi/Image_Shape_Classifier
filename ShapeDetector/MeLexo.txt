Klasifikuesi i formave nga:
Author: Fitim Halimi

Si fillim zgjidhja duhet te behet "build" "Sistem operativ Linux". 
You have to build the solution with Linux operating System.

Pasi te behet build (After you build the program):

In the folder named: Results, you will have the classified images.
Ne folderin "result" dalin rezultated e imazheve te klasifikuara.

In the folder named "test", the images you want  to classify are placed. You can acquire the images depending on your needs.
Ne folderin "test" gjenden te gjitha imazhet qe do te hyjn ne proces per detektim te formave dhe klasifikim,
ne rastin tim kam nxjerre disa modele qe me jane pershtatur ne detyren e dhene.

In the folder named "src" you have the source codes.
Ne folderin "src" gjenden kodet per detektim te formave dhe klasifikimin e tyre.
